How We Make Money

100% transparent. No hidden fees.

Our Commission Model

Krayn earns a small commission from suppliers when you book equipment through our platform. This commission is already built into the supplier's price—you don't pay anything extra.

In fact, our users save an average of 15% compared to booking directly because:

  • Suppliers compete for your business with competitive pricing
  • You get access to bulk discounts and special offers
  • Our AI finds the most cost-effective options for your needs
  • You avoid markup from brokers and middlemen

Pricing FAQs

Can I try Krayn before committing?

Yes! The Individual plan is free forever. For Enterprise features, we offer a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time. No long-term contracts, no cancellation fees.

Do rental prices on Krayn include your commission?

Yes, the prices shown are what you'll pay. Our commission is already included and comes from the supplier's side—you never pay extra for using Krayn.
